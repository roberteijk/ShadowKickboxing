Every download a different training.

Robert's shadow kickboxing generator makes sure that every mp3 download is unique. Choose your settings, press the download button and you will be served a tailored training experience generated specially for you. Time after time. So, you can train with aim and purpose.

Pro tip: Follow the audio instructions in front of a mirror or boxing bag to get even more out of it. This will make a difference for sure.

Development of this generator.

Note that this generator is still in beta. Updates and changes will be relative frequent. Some downtime and small issues should be expected. Please feel free to report any issues or requests new features.

Reasons for this generator.

There are 2 general reasons for this shadow kickboxing mp3 generator:

1) For fun and practice. I wanted a unique training experience when training on my own. Once I created the bare bones, I decided to share this experience with the world.

2) As a practice project for working with the Spring framework while combining Java, JavaScript, jQuery, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, MVC, JPA, Docker and MySQL, among others. Everything is served from a Linux environment.
